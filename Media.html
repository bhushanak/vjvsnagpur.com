Our Special School serves as a nurturing haven for children up to the age of 18, focusing on the critical years of development. We understand that every child learns differently, which is why our curriculum is designed to meet the unique cognitive and emotional needs of students with intellectual challenges. Through a combination of sensory activities, basic academic learning and behavioral therapy, we aim to foster self-reliance and social confidence. Our dedicated educators work closely with each child to unlock their hidden potential, ensuring they have a supportive environment to grow, play, and learn at their own pace.
