Workshop

Transitioning into adulthood brings new challenges, and our Vocational Workshop for individuals above 18 years is designed to turn those challenges into opportunities. Here, our students move beyond the classroom to learn tangible craft skills, such as making candles, greeting cards, or hand-woven items. This program is more than just a creative outlet; it is a path to dignity and financial empowerment. By producing high-quality goods that are sold in the market, our students experience the pride of earning their own income, proving that with the right training, they can contribute meaningfully to the economy and lead a life of purpose.
