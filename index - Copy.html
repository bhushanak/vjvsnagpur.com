Empowering Lives Through Specialized Care & Social Action

Our organization stands at the intersection of specialized education and grassroots social development. From providing residential care for intellectually disabled girls in Nagpur to facilitating integrated human development across 120 villages, we are dedicated to the poor and marginalized.
